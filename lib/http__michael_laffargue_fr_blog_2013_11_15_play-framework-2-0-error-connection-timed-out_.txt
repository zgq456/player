Solution Ensure your firewall isn’t blocking the requests You’re certainly behind a proxy and you need to configure it Modify the last line of the build.bat file, adding those options to the java command. Modifying the variables :MyProxyAccount,MyProxyPwd,MyProxyIp,MyProxyPort to suit your needs -Dhttp.proxyUser=<MyProxyAccount>
-Dhttp.proxyPassword=<MyProxyPwd>
-Dhttp.proxyHost=<MyProxyIp>
